Our Story
We are not just building businesses. We are building a reputation that outlasts us.

Patanwala Group was established in 2024 with a clear conviction: that the way you build matters as much as what you build. Starting with construction and trade, we set out to prove that you can run a tight, commercially sound operation without cutting corners — on quality, on ethics, or on the environment.

We take on projects we believe in, work with partners we trust, and hold ourselves to standards that go beyond the brief. Our ambitions are growing, and so is our footprint — but never faster than our ability to do things right.

2024
Patanwala Group founded. Construction and import-export operations commenced.
2025
Sustainable practices integrated across all operations. Green construction standards adopted as company baseline.
2026
Patanwala Group website launched. Expanding scope across sectors with a green-forward outlook.
Beyond
New ventures in development. Our next chapter will go further — in reach, in impact, and in purpose.
