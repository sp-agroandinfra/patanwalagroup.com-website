One Vision

The Patanwala Group is a forward-looking business conglomerate based in Pune, Maharashtra — built on a foundation of integrity, diligence, and long-term vision.

Operating at the intersection of agriculture and infrastructure, two pillars of India's growth story, the group is committed to building ventures that create real, lasting value for communities, clients, and partners alike.

Led by Director Sahil Patanwala, the group's flagship venture, SP Agro & Infra, brings an entrepreneurial approach to sectors essential to India's future.

🌱

Rooted in Purpose

Every venture under the Patanwala Group is built with intention — to serve communities, empower partners, and generate sustainable growth for the long term.

🏛

Grounded in Pune

Operating from Gagan Ela Commercial Complex in Pune, the group is well-positioned at the heart of Maharashtra's commercial and agricultural ecosystem.
